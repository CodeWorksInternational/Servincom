About Us

Servincom:

Established in 1969, Servincom started working on exporting furniture to Switzerland and manufactured glass-ware to France. Additionally, fruits and vegetables were exported to Jordan, Lebanon and Syria. After the 1973 war, Servincom imported bananas from Ecuador via French companies. For more than five years, Servincom was the sole importer in Egypt. Later in the 70’s, Servincom started importing Hochland cheese from Heimenkirch, Germany. In the late 80’s, the company focused on the agricultural business, concentrating on milk and meat productions where we imported special cattle breeds from Holland. Servincom has been a family owned business for more than three generations and is well-known for its reliability, honesty, teamwork and winning passion.




All Agency for Business Solutions and Exports:

Established in 2014, All Agency presents business solutions and services in the form of marketing, management, and consulting. In 2015 All Agency started exporting agricultural products of all types (fresh, frozen and canned) to the Middle East, Russia and the European Union. In 2016 All Agency signed agreements with several factories and packing stations, all of which are certified and qualified to meet international standards.

Our Mission

After a joint venture with All agency, Servincom decided to provide Egyptian and international markets with the highest quality of fruits and vegetables. With the ability to export products to Europe, the Middle East, Asia and Africa, we aim to provide consultancy to local growers, and high quality packaging adhering to our clients’ requests. Servincom aims to provide international markets with competitive pricing and shipping services by air, sea and land. Our vision is to set up a global network of clients and to have our products reach the entire world.
